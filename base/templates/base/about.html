About Me

Journey

Born in Bajhang, grew up in Dhangadhi, Kailali, and currently living in Kathmandu. Completed B.E. in Geomatics Engineering from Western Regional College. Started career as field surveyor for land use planning and topographic surveys, then worked as GIS Analyst at Mappers Nepal Pvt. Ltd.

Focus

Local governments: Resource mapping, Land use classification and planning, Surveying. Passionate about applying GIS and spatial technologies to solve real-world problems.

Skills

GIS Analysis, Remote Sensing, WebGIS Development (Django + Leaflet), Spatial Data Visualization, Land Use Mapping, Topographic Surveying, Disaster Risk Assessment.

Projects

- Land use & cadastral maps for local governments.
- Disaster risk mapping for hazard-prone areas.
- WebGIS applications for schools, roads, cadastre.
- Topographic surveys for roads, hospitals, and complex buildings.
- Transmission Line survey using DGPS

Hobbies & Interests

Trekking, reading history books, and watching movies.

Future Goals

Master WebGIS, Spatial ML, and environmental GIS applications to support data-driven decisions.
